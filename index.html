About Chicago Capital

$CICAL is a token on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) with a total supply of 1,000,000,000. Transaction fees are structured as follows:

  • 🔥 1% Burn (stops at 10,000,000 tokens)
  • 💎 1% Reflection to holders
  • 💰 1% Dev / Fee wallet for community and growth

Part of the collected fees are used for investments. Returns from these investments are used to buy back tokens, fund airdrops, increase liquidity, support staking rewards, marketing, and other community growth activities.

CICAL Growth Loop

1️⃣ Transaction fees collected → partially invested.
2️⃣ Returns from investments → redistributed to holders, liquidity pool, airdrops, staking, and marketing.
3️⃣ This creates a sustainable growth loop that benefits the community while increasing token value and liquidity.
